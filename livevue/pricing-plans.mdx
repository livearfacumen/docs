## Overview

LIVEVUE offers four subscription tiers so you can start free and scale as your business grows. All paid plans include a **14-day free trial** — no charge until the trial ends.

## One-time Payment

For custom setup, professional services, or invoice-based billing, use the one-time payment option in the **Pricing Plans** screen.

1. Enter your **Invoice Number** provided by the LIVEARf team
2. Enter the **Amount** in USD
3. Click **Pay** to complete the payment

<Info>
  One-time pricing is used for service invoices issued by LIVEARf. If you have not received an invoice number, contact **cs@livearf.com** before proceeding.
</Info>
