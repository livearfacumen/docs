## What is LIVEVUE?

LIVEVUE is LIVEARf's flagship Shopify plugin that gives your customers a **real-time, interactive framing and visualization experience** directly on your product pages — no separate app, no redirects.

Customers can select sizes, frames, mats, and print types and see the result update live before they buy.
